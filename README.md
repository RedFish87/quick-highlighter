# Quick Highlighter
Simple, shortcut-based highlighter with four colours.

## How it works
Select any text, tap H, done. Hold H to choose from 4 colours and release to apply the chosen colour. To remove a highlight, double-click it or press R. When you're ready, press C to copy everything to your clipboard in reading order, one highlight per line.

## Shortcuts
- H -> Yellow highlight
- H (hold) -> Choose from Yellow, Green, Red, or Blue
- R -> Undo last highlight (or double-click any highlight to remove it)
- C -> Copy all highlights to clipboard

## Permissions
Quick Highlighter listens to keyboard events, reads and modifies the text of pages you visit in order to display highlights, and requires clipboard access to copy them. It runs entirely on your local machine and stores nothing.
